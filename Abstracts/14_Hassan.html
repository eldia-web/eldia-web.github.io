A Mathematical Model For Multi-Component Pfas Solute Transport In Unsaturated

Presenter: Hassan Saleem1
Co-Author(s): -
Advisor(s): Dr. Hoshin Gupta and Dr. Bo Guo
1Department of Hydrology and Atmospheric Sciences, University of Arizona


Poster PDF
Poster Session 2

Little is known about behavior when multicomponent PFAS is present in the fluid-fluid interfacial adsorption, although PFAS retention in the vadose zone is decreased in part by competitive adsorption at fluid-fluid interfaces. We present a mathematical model to forecast interfacial tension and air-water interfacial adsorption for multicomponent PFAS and show how, at high concentration, the convolution in space and time is significantly changed.
