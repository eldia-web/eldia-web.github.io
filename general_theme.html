El Día 2023 General Theme

This has been a challenging year for HAS and we look forward to gathering the community together. This year’s El Día - titled "Looking Back and Moving Forward" - It is going to be a more interactive and inclusive event. This year, when we come together for the annual El Día event, we want to take the opportunity to reflect, connect, and imagine. As such, this year’s theme will be Looking Back and Moving Forward. It is going to be a more interactive and inclusive event through which we want to consider the past, present,and future of Hydrology and Atmospheric Sciences as a connected field and department. The event will host a combination of research presentation and collaborative discussions. See link for more information.

In the afternoon, a World (Global) Café will be held to bring students and every attendee together to discuss a broad and subjective topic—such as "the Future of HAS" This will esentially form a simple, effective, and flexible format for hosting large group dialogue thereby helping to shape and to identify the future of the nexus of the hydrologic and atmospheric sciences.
